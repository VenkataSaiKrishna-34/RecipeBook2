Paneer Popcorn


Cooking Time:

Preparation Time : 10Cook Time : 10 Total Time : 20


Ingredients:(Serves 4)
  • Paneer 200grms
  • Turmeric 1tsp
  • Red chilly powder 1tsp
  • Coriander powder 1/2tsp
  • Cumin powder 1/2tsp
  • Chaat masala 2tsp
  • Ginger garlic paste 1tsp
  • Lemon 1/2
  • Bread crumbs 1cup
  • Corn flour 4tbsp
  • Gram flour 2tbsp
  • Salt 2tsp
  • Oil 1 cup

Directions:

  1. Cut the paneers into cubes.
  2. Add all the dry masalas and nicely mix it.
  3. Now add ginger garlic paste, lemon juice and salt .
  4. Now add 1tbsp of gram flour and mix so that the masalas coats the paneers well.
  5. Now make a batter mixing gram flour, corn flour and water.
  6. Dip paneer into the batter one by one and roll them into bread crumbs.
  7. Now fry the paneers till golden brown.
  8. Tip(keep the bread crumbs in the fridge for 1 hour to get nice crunchy texture).
