Mathi Mulakitathu(Sardines in Spicy Curry)


Cooking Time:

Preparation Time : 30Cook Time : 10 Total Time : 40


Ingredients:(Serves 4)
  • Mathi (Sardines,Cleaned and Washed) 1/2 kgs
  • Fenu Greek 1/2 tsp
  • Pearl Onions 15 nos
  • Garlic Cloves 6 nos
  • Curry leaves 1 sprig
  • Tomato(sliced) 1 nos
  • Salt some to taste 1 tsp
  • Tamarind(Lemon Sized diluted in water) 1 nos
  • Water 1 cups
  • Kashmiri Chilly Powder 1 tbsp
  • 2-3 nos
  • Coconut Oil 3 tbsp

Directions:

  1. First of all,mix tamarind,kashmiri chilly powder and turmeric powder in a cup of water and keep it aside.
  2. In a pan,heat oil and add fenugreek to it, and saute the mix by adding the pearl onions , garlic cloves,ginger.
  3. To this mix add the tomato and also the cleaned sardines(mathi) along with required salt .
  4. Add the spicy mix which prepared in the first step and boil it.
  5. When the gravy gets thickened,add the curry leaves and take out of flame.
  6. In a small vessel,crush green chillies,pearl onions in coconut oil.
  7. Mix this with curry well. And its ready to serve.
  8. Serves delicious with Rice
