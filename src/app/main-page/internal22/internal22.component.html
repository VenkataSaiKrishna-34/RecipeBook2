Everest Chole Masala


Cooking Time:

Preparation Time : 10Cook Time : 10 Total Time : 20


Ingredients:(Serves 2)
  • Boiled chana 2 cups
  • Chop onion 2 nos
  • Garlic paste 1 tsp
  • Chop ginger 1 tsp
  • Tomato puree 1/2 cups
  • Salt..As taste 1 tsp
  • Everest chana masala 1 tsp
  • Red chilli 1/2 tsp
  • Haldi 1/2 tsp
  • Oil 3 tsp

Directions:

  1. Take a wok add oil, add garlic saute then add ginger and onion saute till brown
  2. Add all masala and tomato puree saute add chana saute two minutes add water boil them.
  3. Serve with boiled rice.
